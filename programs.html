Programs

Scholarships

Tuition assistance for Christian education (K–12, higher education, adult discipleship). Priority for seniors and families affected by disability.

Assistive Grants

Mini‑grants for devices and services that improve independence and access (screen readers, mobility aids, hearing supports, training).

Community Inclusion

Small grants and training for churches to host accessible, welcoming programs for seniors and people of every ability.
