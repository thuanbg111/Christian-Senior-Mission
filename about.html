About Us

Christian Senior Mission (incorporated 2020) is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law for public and charitable purposes.

Specific purpose: to support Christian education and the disabled through scholarships, assistive grants, and community inclusion programs.

As a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation, Christian Senior Mission is tax-exempt under U.S. federal law. Our organization is officially registered with EIN: 85-3527794. This designation ensures that all contributions are fully tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law, supporting transparency and accountability in every program we run.

  • Public & charitable purposes (Article 4)
  • No political campaigning or private inurement
  • Assets dedicated to charitable, educational, and/or religious purposes
